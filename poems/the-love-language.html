The Love Language

You asked me to speak without a sound, 

To show a love, but on new ground. 

You said, "Don't use those words you know, 

Just let your true affection show."

So I will listen to your day, 

And chase your every fear away. 

I'll bring you coffee, just how you like it, 

My quiet actions, I will strike it.

My love for you is not a phrase, 

But woven into all my days.
