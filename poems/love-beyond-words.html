Love Beyond Words

You are my sunshine every day,

Making life a happy way.

You changed me with just a smile,

Now my heart beats all the while.

I love you more with each new light,

You make my life feel just right.

In your eyes, my heart finds rest,

With you, I am at my best.

I want to tell you how I feel,

But words are hard, and it's hard to reveal.

You mean the world to me, and it's true,

I love you more than words can do.
