Truly Real

I see you sitting over there,

The sunlight shining on your hair.

I want to tell you how I feel,

And that my love is truly real.

I want to hold your hand so tight, 

And be with you all day and night. 

I want to say "I love you so," 

And that I'll never let you go.

So now I'm walking up to you, 

To say these words I know are true. 

You smile and take my hand in yours, 

My happy heart completely soars.
