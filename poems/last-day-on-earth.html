Last day on Earth

The sun will set, the day will end,

My time on earth will soon transcend.

I'll say goodbye to friends so dear,

And wipe away each falling tear.

The world will keep on spinning round,

Life will go on, without a sound.

I'll leave behind my final sigh,

And drift into the evening sky.
