The Feeling of Us

My mind's calm and heart's still, you are the one I need,

Feeling fulfilled, with you is my heart's home indeed.

My world feels more peaceful, more quiet, 

With you by my side, everything feels alright.

My eyes ain't heavy no more, they sparkle and they shine, 

Your presence everywhere, it's just the sweetest sign.

You're my calm, my guiding light, 

With you, everything's alright.
