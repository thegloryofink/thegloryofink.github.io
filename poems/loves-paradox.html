Love's Paradox

I'm with you, but I'm alone, 

Together forever, but never home.

We're far apart, but very close too,

Missing you, but feeling you.

I'm lost with you, but I'm found, 

n your eyes, but never around.

Our love's a yes, but it's a no,

Forever with you, but I don't know.

I'm full of you, but I'm empty too,

Having you, but not having you.

You're always mine, but never here,

Forever near, but far from near.
